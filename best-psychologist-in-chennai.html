What a psychologist does

A psychologist helps a person understand thoughts, feelings, and behavior in a structured way. That can be useful when the mind feels full, confusing, tired, or stuck. Good counseling does not rush the person. It helps them sort out one thing at a time.

In simple terms, it is like having a trained guide who helps you make sense of what is happening and what can be done next.
