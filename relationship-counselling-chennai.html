What relationship counselling is

Relationship counselling gives people a calm place to talk about repeated misunderstandings, trust problems, communication issues, and emotional distance. It helps slow things down so the real problem can be understood.

The goal is not to decide who is right or wrong. The goal is to help people hear each other better and stop the same painful pattern from repeating.

ELI5 version

Think of a relationship like two people carrying the same box. If one goes left and the other goes right, the box becomes harder to carry. Counseling helps both people face the same direction first.

Once the direction is clearer, the conversation usually becomes easier and less stressful.

Who this page helps

  • Couples having repeated arguments
  • People who feel misunderstood or unheard
  • Partners dealing with trust or boundary issues
  • People who want a calmer way to talk and decide

What to expect in support

Sessions usually focus on listening, slowing the conversation, and helping both sides explain the real issue underneath the argument. That makes it easier to find practical next steps.

Support can happen in Chennai or online depending on comfort and convenience.

Frequently asked questions

Can couples counseling help?

Yes. It can improve communication and decision-making.

Is online couples support available?

Yes. Online options can work well for many couples.

Do both people need to agree first?

Ideally yes, but sometimes one person starts and the other joins later.
