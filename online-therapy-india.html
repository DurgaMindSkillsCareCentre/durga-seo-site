Why online therapy works

Online therapy is useful for people who want privacy, convenience, and continuity. It lets the client receive support without travel, which can make it easier to stay consistent.

For many people, it is not a second choice. It is the most practical way to begin and continue support.

ELI5 version

Think of it like a safe meeting room that comes to your home through a screen. The support is still professional, but the travel part is removed.

That is helpful when someone is busy, lives outside Chennai, or simply feels more comfortable starting from home.
